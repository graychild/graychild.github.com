Summer didn't use to be much of a car seat fan, but her attitude to travel seems to be improving. That just proves there's nothing a little torture can't fix. Lock a baby in a box for four days of travel so Dad can give a 45 minute speech and they'll be forced to discover the bright side. Who says you can't teach babies?
