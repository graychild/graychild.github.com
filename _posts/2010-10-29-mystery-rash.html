by James

A Pediatrician once told Dana and I, "Rashes are the bane of my existence." Today they were Summer's too.

This one came on quick, only below the knees, and was already fading before we could decide if we should bother the doctor. It looks like the Human Immune Fighters have secured another victory against the Evil League of Germs.

Alternately, it may have just been light bruising from too tight socks. She's growing so fast now her Mom and I can barely keep up with what still fits!
