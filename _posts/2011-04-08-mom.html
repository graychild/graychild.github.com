"Dada" is suppose to be easier than "Mama," and both of those easier than "Mom" or "Dad." Of course, we're now use to Summer preferring to take the hard way first.

Summer has babbled "Dada" and "Mama" for a while, sometimes with extra syllables. However, we haven't really noticed her addressing us directly with these terms.

Starting on Mommy's birthday though, she seems to have upgraded to "Mom" and we've even seen her try to use it to get Mommy's attention a couple of times. It doesn't seem flawless yet, as we've also seen her referring to her stuffed bunny as "Mom." Still, it was a pretty nice birthday present.
