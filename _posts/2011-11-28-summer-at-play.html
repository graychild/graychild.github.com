--- title: Summer at Play layout: post ---
  • It's great to just watch Summer at play…
  • She loves books and spends a lot of time with them.
  • Sometimes she reads them herself.
  • Sometimes she brings them to us to read.
  • She can get fixated on certain toys, wanting to figure them out.
  • She can concentrate pretty hard when needed.
  • She always seems curious about us.
  • She loves to go for a ride in the laundry basket.
  • You often find her climbing into strange places.
  • She'll just sit there…
  • And play.
  • She often works in some relaxation time in her chair (but we bribed the dog.)
  • She finally figured out the peek-a-boo game.
  • She makes sure we don't get more sleep than strictly needed.
  • She loves to tell us about her day.
  • She enjoys seeing the sites.
  • She can usually entertain herself, for a bit, while we work.
  • She is already learning from Grandma. (That can't be good!)
  • She forces us to put this too-small hat on her head.
  • Perhaps she likes it because Mommy made it.
  • Oh, and there was recently an ice cream incident…
  • Daddy swears he is innocent, even if that is his ice cream on her cheeks.
  • Most of all, Summer is a happy girl who shares that with us.
