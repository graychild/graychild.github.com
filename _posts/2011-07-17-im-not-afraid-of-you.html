by James

I didn't manage to catch this episode on film, but it's too funny not to share.

Summer and Kaylee (our cat) both love to play in the cardboard tunnel Dana built. Sometimes at the same time.

Well, I guess Kaylee felt a little threatened or cornered by the baby inside the tunnel. Mommy and I heard the hiss and then she bounded off.

Summer thought about it for a second, then chased that cat all over our house mimicing Kaylee's hiss the whole time. Mommy and I were laughing too hard to rescue the cat and Summer must have chased her for a good ten minutes.

I guess we know who is queen of this castle now!
