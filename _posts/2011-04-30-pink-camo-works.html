by James

As a skeptical dad, I questioned the value of pink camouflage. I even asked on Twitter, "What you are suppose to hide in?" Some responses were:

  • A giant vat of cotton candy
  • Life size dollhouses
  • A gaggle of tween girls
  • Or Miami

But the joke is on all of us. As you can clearly see, it actually works!
