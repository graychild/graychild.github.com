Summer got her one year checkup from the doctor. She passed a vision check and the doctor thinks she's cutting her one year molars now. We've started giving her cold teethers again and it seems to be helping.

She's still pretty low in weight classifications at under the 25 percentile. However, that reading may have been affected a bit by her recent bout with the childhood illness Hand, Foot, and Mouth Disease. She didn't eat for almost week while battling that, though she's feeling much better now.

Height has also tapered off some to the 75 percentile. It's still a bit early to tell, but they estimate she would be about 5'6" as an adult, if she holds to this current growth rate.

All in all, it's a clean bill of health. Hooray!
